the internets busiest music nerd

Anthony Fantano (/fænˈtænoʊ/; born October 28, 1985) is an American music critic, video producer and journalist best known for creating The Needle Drop, a music-related video blog. He is self-described as "the internet's busiest music nerd". Fantano's website and YouTube channel review a variety of music genres, including pop, hip hop, rock, metal, indie, electronic, folk, jazz and experimental.
